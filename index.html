About Me

Hi! my name is Zane Pemberton, i was born in inner city Melbourne, but grew up in country Victoria. I left high school with zero plans of ever working on a computer, I left school with a cert 3 in building & construction and a year 12 pass and i thought that would be my career for life. But after a few years i realized this was not something i wanted to do for the rest of my life. So after 4 years i became a qualified tradesman and then quit a week later to explore new things. Here i am now, ready to explore the world of code and development. I hope to take the learnt skills from this course and use them to create and hopefully run my own e-commerce store sucsessfully but i would be more than happy to start a career in web development.
